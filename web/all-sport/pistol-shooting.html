Pistol-Shooting Information


Shooting sports is a group of competitive and recreational sporting activities involving proficiency tests of accuracy, precision and speed in shooting — the art of using ranged weapons, mainly small arms (firearms and airguns, in forms such as handguns, rifles and shotguns) and bows/crossbows.

RULES

Rule 1 – Treat Every Firearm as if it is Loaded.

Rule 2 – Always Keep the Muzzle Pointed in a Safe Direction.

Rule 3 – Keep Your Finger Off the Trigger Until Ready to Shoot.

Rule 4 – Know Your Target and What's Beyond It.

Rule 5 – Secure Firearms and Ammunition Safely.

NSF BODY

The National Rifle Association of India (NRAI)


The National Rifle Association of India (NRAI) is the governing body for the sport of pistol shooting in India. It is responsible for promoting and developing shooting sports across the country, organizing competitions, and managing the selection of national teams. The NRAI also ensures that all competitions adhere to international standards set by the International Shooting Sport Federation (ISSF).
