Archery Information


Archery is the sport, practice, or skill of using a bow to shoot arrows.it is mainly a competitive sport and recreational activity. A person who practices archery is typically called an archer, bowman, or toxophilite.

Measurement

RULES

Safety - Always point your bow and arrow in a safe direction, and only shoot at your target.

Range etiquette - Always walk on the range, and give other archers space.

Equipment - Check your bow and arrows for damage before each use. Wear protective gear.

Range commands - Know and obey all range commands such as whistle blows.

Other rules - Keep your arrow in your quiver until instructed to shoot.

NSF BODY

Archery Association of India (AAI)


The Archery Association of India (AAI) is the governing body for the sport of archery in India. It organizes national competitions, promotes the sport at all levels, and ensures adherence to international standards.
